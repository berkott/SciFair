How it works

This app syncs with the Muse 2 to see if you have sleep apnea, all from the comfort of your home.

What you have to do

For one night before you sleep, put on the Muse and connect it to the Muse Direct app. Then once you wake up complete the short post sleep questionnaire. Thats it!
