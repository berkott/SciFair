If you have any questions please contact us at berkott3002@gmail.com. How to use the Muse To use the Muse 2 hold the power button until the light starts flashing. At that point, select your device from the Muse Direct app. The simply press the record button seen on the graph page. How to use the Apnea App This app shows you your Apnea score for each night you sleep with the Muse 2. In order to get your data, you must first complete the post sleep questionnaire found on the side menu. The logout button is also found on the side menu. Security This app was created using Mongodb Stitch, a HIPAA certified backend service. This means all of the data you give and see on this app is all secure. You doctor will also be able to see your data. Muse Direct (the app to record a session) on the other hand, is not HIPAA certified. This means that this app would not be usable in a professional environment. Currently Muse has not made it possible to get the data in a way that is HIPAA certified but a new SDK is being developed and will soon give us the opportunity to develop a HIPAA certified method for transmitting sleep data.
